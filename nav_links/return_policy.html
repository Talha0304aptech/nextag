Returns Policy

At NEXTAG, we strive to ensure your utmost satisfaction with every purchase you make from us. We understand that sometimes you may need to return or exchange an item, and we want to make the process as smooth and convenient as possible. Please take a moment to review our Returns Policy below:

Eligibility for Returns:

  • We accept returns within 30 days of the purchase date.
  • The item must be unused, in its original condition, and with all tags and packaging intact.
  • Personalized or customized items cannot be returned unless they are defective or damaged.

Return Process:

  • To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team via email or phone. We will provide you with a return authorization number and detailed instructions.
  • Ensure the item is securely packaged to prevent damage during transit.
  • Ship the item back to us using a trackable and insured shipping method.
  • Include the return authorization number on the package or within the package for faster processing.

Refund Options:

  • Once we receive the returned item and verify its condition, we will process your refund.
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used during the purchase.
  • Please note that shipping fees, if any, are non-refundable.
  • It may take up to business days for the refund to reflect in your account, depending on your payment provider.

Exchange Process:

  • If you would like to exchange an item for a different color, style, or size, please contact our customer service team.
  • We will assist you with the exchange process and provide further instructions.
  • Please note that exchange requests are subject to product availability.

Damaged or Defective Items:

  • In the rare event that you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer service team immediately.
  • We will guide you through the return process and arrange for a replacement or refund.
  • Please provide clear photos or detailed information regarding the damage or defect for our records.

Non-Returnable Items:

  • Certain items are non-returnable for hygiene and safety reasons, such as earrings, intimate apparel, and skincare products.
  • Gift cards or promotional items are also non-returnable and non-refundable.

International Returns:

  • For international customers, please note that return shipping fees and any applicable customs duties or taxes are the responsibility of the customer.
  • We recommend using a reputable shipping service with tracking and insurance for international returns.

Please feel free to reach out to our customer service team with any further questions or concerns. We are here to assist you throughout the returns process and ensure your complete satisfaction with your shopping experience. NEXTAG reserves the right to update or modify this Returns Policy at any time without prior notice.
